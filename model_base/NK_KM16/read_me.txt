NK_KM16 Replication

+ last change: 18-09-2018

Current folder can be used to replicate the results of Krause and Moyen (2016) - "Public Debt and Changing Inflation Targets".

+ replication.mod reproduces 
	- the solid blue line in Figure 2 of the paper (debt shock);
	- the dashed blue line in Figure 4 of the paper (inflation target shock);

+ literature:
  - Krause and Moyen (2016) - "Public Debt and Changing Inflation Targets", AEJ:Macroeconomics 2016, 8(4), 
	142-176
